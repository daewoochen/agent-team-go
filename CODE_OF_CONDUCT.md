# Code of Conduct

We are committed to building an open, welcoming, and respectful community.

## Our standards

- Be kind and constructive
- Assume good intent
- Give actionable feedback
- Focus on helping builders succeed

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment or discrimination
- Personal attacks
- Publishing private information without consent
- Repeated hostile or dismissive communication

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, or contributions that violate this code of conduct.

For concerns, please contact the maintainers through a private security or moderation channel.
